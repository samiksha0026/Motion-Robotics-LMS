# Video Instructions

## For Experiment #6 Demo Video

Place your buzzer experiment demo video files here:

### Required Files:

- `experiment-6-buzzer-demo.mp4` (H.264 encoded video)
- `experiment-6-buzzer-demo.webm` (WebM encoded video for better browser compatibility)

### Video Requirements:

- **Resolution**: 1280x720 (720p) or higher
- **Duration**: 2-5 minutes
- **Content**: Should show:
  1. Components laid out clearly
  2. Wiring connections being made
  3. Testing the circuit operation
  4. Expected buzzer sound/behavior
  5. Final working demonstration

### Format Guidelines:

- **MP4**: H.264 video codec, AAC audio codec
- **WebM**: VP9 video codec, Opus audio codec
- **Recommended bitrate**: 2-4 Mbps for good quality
- **Audio**: Clear narration or background music (optional)

### Upload Instructions:

1. Place the video files in this `/Frontend/public/videos/` folder
2. The experiment page will automatically detect and display the video for experiment #6
3. Students will see this video in the "Demo Video" tab when they open experiment #6

### Future Videos:

- Name pattern: `experiment-{number}-{description}-demo.{format}`
- Update the experiment page component to include checks for other experiment IDs as needed
